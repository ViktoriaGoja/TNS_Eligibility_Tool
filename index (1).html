You will receive a flyer from the DMV agent explaining how to pay for and receive your physical license.
The DMV agent will issue a temporary license valid for 20 days — purchase your real license the next day as it takes 5–7 business days to arrive.
No further paperwork or contact with TNS is required.

If passed — MA
If RMV fees are prepaid through the myRMV portal at mass.gov, the RMV examiner will sign the learner's permit, which becomes the temporary license starting the next day.
The official license will be mailed to the address on the road test application form that was submitted.
Make sure fees are paid in advance via myRMV at mass.gov before the test date.
✗
If failed — MA
Must wait 2 weeks (14 days) from the test date before retesting.
To retest with TNS, purchase the test through the Student Portal.
Student must pay the RMV retest fee in the myRMV portal at mass.gov before TNS can book the next road test.
